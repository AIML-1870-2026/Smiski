BLACKJACK

The Object

Beat the dealer's hand without going over 21. Get closer to 21, or watch the dealer go bust.

Card Values

  • 2 – 10  → face value
  • J / Q / K → 10
  • Ace      → 1 or 11

Your Moves

  • HIT — take another card
  • STAND — hold your hand
  • DOUBLE — 2× bet, one card
  • SPLIT — split a matching pair
  • INSURANCE — side bet vs dealer BJ

Payouts

  • Win → 1 : 1
  • Blackjack → 3 : 2
  • Insurance → 2 : 1
  • Push → bet returned
  • Hit $0 → things get personal
